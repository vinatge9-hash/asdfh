About Niyantri Cafe

We started with a simple belief: great coffee, warm conversations, and a space that feels like home. Our beans are sourced from local growers near Hyderabad, roasted with care, and brewed to bring a comforting hug in a cup.

Our Story

From a evenings chat over a shared latte to opening a neighborhood staple, Niyantri Cafe grew from a love of quiet mornings and good company.

Sourced with Care

We partner with local farmers for beans and seasonal treats that celebrate our community.
